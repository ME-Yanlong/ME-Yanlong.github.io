I am currently a PhD candidate at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and a member of the Neural-Symbolic Artificial Intelligence Community (NSAIHOME) led by Professor Ming Chen.

I received my bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Wuhan University of Technology in 2023, together with a first-class bachelor’s degree in engineering from its partner university in the United Kingdom. In the same year, I was admitted to Shanghai Jiao Tong University through a recommended postgraduate admission pathway and began my doctoral studies.

My homepage highlights my research interests, publications, and academic activities in embodied intelligence and industrial manufacturing.

Research Interests

My research interests span neural-symbolic artificial intelligence, embodied intelligent robotics, and green manufacturing. More specifically, I am interested in building dual-system intelligence for embodied robotic platforms, including collaborative robots, industrial robots, and humanoid robots. I explore this through task and motion planning with explicit knowledge alignment, as well as robot-oriented neural information networks with implicit knowledge alignment.
